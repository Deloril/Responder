PROJECT OVERVIEW
----------------
Establishment of The Secrets Group's first Asia-Pacific hub office in Singapore
to serve growing demand from regional financial services and technology clients.
The office will provide sales, client services, and technical support for the
SSN platform across Singapore, Malaysia, Indonesia, and Hong Kong markets.

BUDGET: SGD 850,000
TIMELINE: January 2026 - June 2026 (6 months)

MILESTONES & TIMELINE
---------------------

M1: Lease Signed (Target: 31 Jan 2026) - COMPLETED
- Location: Marina Bay Financial Centre, Tower 2, Level 18
- Floor area: 420 sqm
- Lease term: 5 years with 2-year break option
- Responsible: Jennifer Li, Hemi Tama

M2: Fit-Out & Build (Target: 28 Feb 2026)
- Interior design and contractor selection
- Partitioning: 4 meeting rooms, 1 boardroom, open plan for 25 workstations
- Secure storage room for physical escrow materials
- Responsible: Jennifer Li
- Budget: SGD 280,000

M3: IT Infrastructure (Target: 15 Mar 2026)
- Network connectivity: dual fibre, 1Gbps committed
- Domain: TSG-INTERNAL.LAB (Singapore site)
- VoIP integration with Wellington HQ
- Secure vault access infrastructure
- Responsible: Michael Huang, Wiremu Sio
- Budget: SGD 95,000

M4: Staff Recruitment (Target: 30 Apr 2026)
- Country Manager (1)
- Client Success Managers (2)
- Technical Support Engineers (2)
- Office Administrator (1)
- Responsible: Sophie Patel (HR), Ava Phan (Technical screening)

M5: Regulatory Approvals (Target: 31 May 2026)
- Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) notification
- Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) compliance review
- Escrow service licensing verification
- Responsible: Jennifer Li, Sophie Patel

M6: Soft Launch (Target: 15 Jun 2026)
- Office operational
- Client onboarding for Singapore-based clients
- Marketing launch event
- Responsible: Jennifer Li, Henry Ngata
