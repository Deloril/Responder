DETAILED GAP ANALYSIS
================================================================================

CC4 - MONITORING (2 Gaps)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
GAP-CC4-01: Continuous monitoring controls for vault access anomalies lack
automated alerting. Current process relies on weekly manual review of logs
by David Nair. Recommendation: Implement SIEM rules for after-hours access,
concurrent session alerts, and failed authentication thresholds.
Remediation: Jennifer Li (IT) | Timeline: 31 Oct 2025

GAP-CC4-02: Management review of control effectiveness (quarterly) not
formally documented with action items and follow-up. Add structured
template and calendar reminders. Remediation: Lisa Liu | Timeline: 15 Oct 2025


CC5 - CONTROL ACTIVITIES (3 Gaps)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
GAP-CC5-01: Segregation of duties - ben holds both vault release authority
and backup key custodian role for Wellington. Mitigate by designating
Priya Rangi as alternate backup custodian with formal handover procedure.
Remediation: Henry Ngata | Timeline: 30 Nov 2025

GAP-CC5-02: Vendor access (Datacom) to production SSN environment - ensure
session recording and time-limited access tokens are implemented. Current
RDP access lacks full audit trail. Remediation: Jennifer Li | Timeline: 15 Nov 2025

GAP-CC5-03: Password policy for service accounts - 12 accounts identified
with passwords exceeding 365-day age. Rotate and document in privileged
access management procedure. Remediation: David Nair | Timeline: 20 Nov 2025


CC6 - LOGICAL ACCESS (1 Gap)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
GAP-CC6-01: MFA enforcement for TSG-INTERNAL.LAB - 3 contractor accounts
(external developers) have conditional access exemptions. Remove exemptions
or implement hardware token requirement. Remediation: Jennifer Li | Timeline: 31 Oct 2025


CC7 - SYSTEM OPERATIONS (2 Gaps)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
GAP-CC7-01: Incident response runbook - last update March 2025. Update to
reflect new Singapore office contact details and Privacy Act 2020 mandatory
breach notification timelines. Remediation: Sophie Patel | Timeline: 30 Nov 2025

GAP-CC7-02: Backup encryption verification - confirm SSN platform backups
stored in AWS S3 are encrypted at rest with KMS and key rotation is
documented. Remediation: David Nair | Timeline: 15 Nov 2025


CC9 - RISK MITIGATION (1 Gap)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
GAP-CC9-01: Cyber insurance policy - ensure coverage limits ($5M) are
documented and aligned with risk register. Provide evidence of annual
review. Remediation: Sarah Wang | Timeline: 15 Dec 2025


A1 - AVAILABILITY (2 Gaps)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
GAP-A1-01: RTO/RPO for SSN platform - documented as 4hr/1hr but DR test
(June 2025) achieved 6.2hr recovery. Update documentation or improve
recovery procedures to meet stated objectives. Remediation: Jennifer Li | Timeline: 30 Nov 2025

GAP-A1-02: Scheduled maintenance window communication - no formal process
for notifying clients of planned outages. Implement 72-hour advance notice
template and client distribution list. Remediation: Kate | Timeline: 15 Nov 2025


PI1 - PROCESSING INTEGRITY (1 Gap)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
GAP-PI1-01: Escrow release verification - add automated checksum validation
to release workflow to ensure deposited materials match released materials.
Currently manual comparison. Remediation: David Nair | Timeline: 30 Nov 2025
