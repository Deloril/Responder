EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
-----------------
The surveillance audit was conducted to verify continued conformity of TSG's
information security management system (ISMS) with ISO 27001:2022. Overall
the ISMS was found to be effectively maintained. Two minor non-conformities
were raised requiring corrective action within 90 days. Five observations and
three opportunities for improvement were documented for management attention.


MINOR NON-CONFORMITIES (2)
================================================================================

NC-2025-001: Access Review Documentation (Annex A.5.15 - Identity Management)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Finding: Quarterly access reviews for vault administrators were not fully
documented for Q2 2025. Evidence of review completion was incomplete for
ben, david.nair, and priya.rangi. The Chief Custodian Officer (Henry Ngata)
confirmed verbal review occurred but signed attestation was not retained.

Requirement: ISO 27001 Clause 9.2; Annex A.5.15 - Access rights shall be
reviewed at defined intervals and documented.

Corrective Action: Implement formal access review checklist with sign-off;
conduct retrospective review for Q2; update procedure VAULT-ACC-003.
Owner: Henry Ngata | Due: 12 February 2026


NC-2025-002: Supplier Security Assessment (Annex A.5.19 - Information Security
             in Project Management)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Finding: Security assessment for Datacom managed services (renewed August 2025)
was not completed prior to contract renewal. Assessment was conducted
retrospectively on 3 September 2025. Procedure SUPPLIER-001 requires
assessment completion before contract signature.

Requirement: ISO 27001 Clause 8.1; Annex A.5.19 - Security requirements shall
be identified and agreed before engaging with suppliers.

Corrective Action: Update procurement workflow to block contract approval
until supplier security assessment is complete; add calendar reminder for
renewals 60 days prior. Owner: Jennifer Li | Due: 15 February 2026


OBSERVATIONS (5)
================================================================================

OBS-2025-01: Singapore office was added to scope in September 2025. Risk
assessment and statement of applicability (SoA) updates for Singapore
operations are in progress. Recommend completion before next surveillance.

OBS-2025-02: SSN platform penetration test (scheduled Q4 2025) results not yet
available at audit. Auditor noted this is a key control for the in-scope
platform. Confirm remediation tracking is established.

OBS-2025-03: Incident response tabletop exercise last conducted March 2025.
Consider increasing frequency to bi-annual given regulatory expectations
(Privacy Act 2020, Notifiable Data Breach scheme).

OBS-2025-04: Documented change management for vault access control system
upgrade (October 2025) could be strengthened with explicit security impact
assessment and rollback procedure documentation.

OBS-2025-05: Backup restoration test for SSN platform data (last performed
June 2025) - ensure next test is documented with success criteria and
evidence of validation.


OPPORTUNITIES FOR IMPROVEMENT (3)
================================================================================

OFI-2025-01: Consider extending ISMS scope to include Sydney and Melbourne
offices in next certification cycle. Current scope excludes these locations
which may create client assurance gaps for Australian clients.

OFI-2025-02: Implement automated evidence collection for access reviews
(integration with Azure AD/Entra ID) to reduce manual documentation burden
and improve audit trail completeness.

OFI-2025-03: Align internal audit schedule with SOC 2 Type II audit timeline
to enable shared evidence and reduce duplicate testing. Sophie Patel (Internal
Audit) and Lisa Liu (Compliance) to coordinate.
