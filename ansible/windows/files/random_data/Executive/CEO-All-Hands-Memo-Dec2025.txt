Kia ora team,

As we approach the end of 2025, I want to take a moment to reflect on what

dedication has been instrumental to our success.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Q4 & FULL-YEAR ACHIEVEMENTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Financial Performance
  - Q4 revenue is tracking ahead of forecast; we expect to close FY2025 at
    approximately NZD 8.6M, representing 11% growth over FY2024
  - Client retention remains strong at 94%; our NPS score has improved to 52
  - We have maintained a healthy cash position with no debt, giving us
    flexibility for strategic investment

Singapore Office
  - I'm delighted to confirm that our Singapore office officially opened on
    1 October. This marks a significant milestone in our APAC expansion
  - Leilani Kumar and her team have done outstanding work establishing our
    presence. MAS registration is progressing and we expect final approval
    in Q1 2026
  - We already have four prospects in advanced discussions—a testament to
    the market opportunity and our team's efforts

SSN Platform
  - Henry and the technology team have delivered SSN Phase 2 to 68%
    completion. We remain on track for general availability in Q1 2026
  - The new API capabilities and multi-vault support will unlock significant
    value for our clients and position us for our SaaS evolution

Operational Excellence
  - Vault uptime: 99.97% year-to-date
  - Zero client-impacting security incidents
  - ISO 27001 surveillance and SOC 2 Type II audits completed successfully

The Board has approved our Strategic Plan for 2026-2028. Key themes for
