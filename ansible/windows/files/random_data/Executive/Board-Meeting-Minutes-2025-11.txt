AGENDA AND PROCEEDINGS
================================================================================

1. WELCOME AND APOLOGIES
   Chair James Whitfield opened the meeting at 09:02. Quorum confirmed.
   No apologies received.

2. CONFIRMATION OF PREVIOUS MINUTES (14 August 2025)
   Motion: That the minutes of the Board meeting held 14 August 2025 be
   confirmed as a true and correct record.
   Moved: Patricia Chen | Seconded: Elena Vasquez
   Vote: Carried unanimously

3. CEO REPORT (Daniel Kemp)
   - Q3 revenue NZD 2.14M, up 11% YoY; on track for FY2025 target
   - Singapore office opened 1 October; MAS registration pending final approval
   - SSN platform Phase 2 development 68% complete; target GA Q1 2026
   - Client retention 94%; NPS score 52 (up from 48)
   - Staff headcount 47 FTE across five offices
   Board noted strong execution. Robert Te Koha asked about cultural
   integration for Singapore team; CEO confirmed local hire Leilani Kumar
   leading with Wellington support.

4. FINANCIAL PERFORMANCE (Kate Morrison)
   - YTD revenue NZD 6.42M; EBITDA margin 18.2%
   - Singapore ramp-up costs NZD 208K YTD as budgeted
   - Cash position NZD 1.82M; no debt
   - FY2025 forecast: revenue NZD 8.6M, EBITDA NZD 1.52M
   Patricia Chen requested sensitivity analysis on Singapore revenue
   assumptions; CFO to circulate by 25 November.

5. SINGAPORE OFFICE UPDATE (Charlotte Mata)
   - Office fit-out complete; 2 local staff onboarded
   - MAS application submitted; expected approval Q1 2026
   - Pipeline: 4 prospects in advanced discussions
   - Risk: PDPA compliance mapping ongoing; external counsel engaged
   Board expressed support. Marcus Webb asked about competitive landscape;
   COO noted SecureVault Pacific and regional banks as key competitors.

6. SSN PLATFORM ROADMAP (Henry Ngata)
   - Phase 2: API banking connectors, multi-vault support, audit logging
   - Phase 3 (2026): SaaS model, self-service provisioning, white-label
   - Security: Penetration test scheduled December; no critical findings
     in internal review
   - Budget request: Additional NZD 85K for Phase 3 architecture design
   Motion: That the Board approve NZD 85,000 for SSN Phase 3 architecture
   design, to be expended in Q1 2026.
   Moved: Elena Vasquez | Seconded: Robert Te Koha
   Vote: Carried unanimously

7. RISK COMMITTEE REPORT (Patricia Chen)
   - Risk Register reviewed; 5 Critical, 6 High risks unchanged
   - Key person dependency (R003): Deputy Custodian designation in progress
   - Singapore regulatory (R016): Mitigation on track
   - New risk R018: M&A integration (preliminary; see item 9)
   Board noted. James Whitfield requested quarterly risk trend reporting.

8. COMPLIANCE UPDATE (Sarah Wang)
   - ISO 27001 surveillance audit: 2 minor non-conformities; corrective
     actions due 15 December
   - SOC 2 Type II: Fieldwork complete; report expected 20 December
   - FMA engagement: No regulatory concerns raised
   - AML/CFT: Annual review completed; procedures updated
   Board acknowledged. No questions.

9. CAPITAL EXPENDITURE - VAULT EXPANSION
   CFO presented business case for Wellington vault expansion:
   - Capacity constraint: Current vault 78% utilised; growth projection
     requires 40% additional capacity by Q3 2026
   - Proposal: Expand adjacent secure zone; total capex NZD 420,000
   - Payback: 3.2 years based on incremental revenue assumptions
   - Funding: Operating cash flow; no debt required
   Motion: That the Board approve capital expenditure of NZD 420,000 for
   Wellington vault expansion, subject to final design sign-off by Chief
   Custodian Officer and CFO.
   Moved: Marcus Webb | Seconded: Patricia Chen
   Vote: Carried unanimously (6-0)
   Action: Fetu Yoon and Kate Morrison to provide design sign-off by
   15 December 2025.

10. OTHER BUSINESS
    - M&A preliminary assessment: Chair advised that a confidential
      paper would be circulated to Board only; discussion deferred to
      December meeting.
    - Next meeting: 16 December 2025, 09:00, Wellington HQ
