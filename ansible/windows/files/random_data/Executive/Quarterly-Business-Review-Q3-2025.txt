EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
-----------------
Q3 2025 delivered solid results with revenue of NZD 2.14M (up 11% YoY) and
EBITDA of NZD 378K (17.7% margin). Singapore office opened on schedule.
SSN Phase 2 development progressed but experienced minor scope creep.
Key risks: talent retention, Singapore regulatory timeline, and Q4 audit
load. Priorities for Q4: close FY2025 strong, complete SOC 2 and ISO
surveillance, and prepare for SSN GA in Q1 2026.
