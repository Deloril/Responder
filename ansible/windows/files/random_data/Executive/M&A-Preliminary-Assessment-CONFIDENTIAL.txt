TARGET OVERVIEW
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Target Company:     SecureVault Pacific Pty Ltd
Headquarters:       Sydney, NSW, Australia
Founded:            2018
Employees:          ~28 FTE
Primary Services:   Digital escrow, document custody, M&A escrow
Key Markets:        Australia, New Zealand (limited)


STRATEGIC RATIONALE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Market Consolidation: SecureVault Pacific holds an estimated 12% share of the
   Australian digital escrow market. Acquisition would immediately establish TSG
   as the leading APAC custodian with combined NZ + AU presence.

2. Client Base: Target maintains ~45 active escrow clients including several
   ASX-listed entities. Client overlap with TSG is minimal (<5%), presenting
   cross-sell opportunity for SSN platform and vault services.

3. Regulatory Footprint: SecureVault holds Australian Financial Services Licence
   (AFSL) and AUSTRAC registration. Acquisition would accelerate TSG's AU
   regulatory readiness and avoid 18-24 month greenfield licensing timeline.

4. Talent: Target's custody and compliance team (8 FTE) has deep AU market
   experience. Retention would be critical to integration success.


PRELIMINARY VALUATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Estimated Enterprise Value:    AUD 12,000,000 - 15,000,000
Valuation Methodology:         EV/Revenue multiple (2.5x-3.0x), DCF sensitivity
Revenue (FY2025E):             AUD 4.2M
EBITDA (FY2025E):              AUD 680K (16% margin)

Valuation Range Drivers:
- Base case (AUD 12M): Conservative multiple, assumes client attrition risk
- Upside case (AUD 15M): Premium for AFSL, clean compliance record, retention


SYNERGIES (ANNUALISED, NZD EQUIVALENT)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Revenue Synergies:
  - Cross-sell SSN platform to SecureVault clients              NZD 180-250K
  - Migrate AU clients to TSG vault network (margin improvement) NZD 90-120K
  - Referral flow from combined sales team                      NZD 60-90K

Cost Synergies:
  - Consolidate IT infrastructure (AWS, security tools)         NZD 95-130K
  - Shared compliance function (AU + NZ)                        NZD 70-95K
  - Eliminate duplicate corporate overhead                      NZD 45-65K

Total Annual Synergies (Year 3):                                NZD 540-750K


RISKS & CONCERNS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Client Retention: 30% of SecureVault revenue from top 3 clients. Key person
   relationships may not transfer. Recommend earn-out structure tied to retention.

2. Integration Complexity: Different tech stack (legacy on-prem vs. TSG cloud).
   Henry Ngata estimates 9-12 month integration timeline for full consolidation.

3. Regulatory: AFSL transfer requires ASIC approval. Historical compliance
   review essential - one minor breach noted in 2023 (remediated).

4. Culture: Target is family-founded; potential resistance to TSG processes.
   Due diligence to assess cultural fit recommended.
