EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
-----------------
This strategic plan positions The Secrets Group for sustained growth across
the Asia-Pacific region, transitioning from a New Zealand-centric escrow
and custodianship provider to a regional leader in digital secret management.
Key pillars: market expansion, platform evolution, vault network growth,
regulatory readiness, and technology modernisation.

================================================================================
PILLAR 1: MARKET EXPANSION (APAC)
================================================================================

Vision: Establish TSG as the preferred digital escrow and secret custodianship
provider across Australia, New Zealand, and Southeast Asia.

FY2026 Initiatives:
  - Singapore: Achieve MAS approval; onboard first 6 paying clients; revenue
    target NZD 380K
  - Australia: Expand Sydney and Melbourne sales presence; target 15% revenue
    growth from Australian clients
  - New Zealand: Defend market position; target enterprise segment (legal,
    M&A, tech) for 20% new client growth

FY2027 Initiatives:
  - Singapore: Scale to 20+ clients; evaluate Hong Kong or Kuala Lumpur
  - Australia: Assess local entity (Pty Ltd) if revenue exceeds NZD 1.2M
  - Regional partnerships: Explore referral arrangements with Big Four
    advisory firms

FY2028 Initiatives:
  - Second Southeast Asian market (Hong Kong or KL) if Singapore profitable
  - APAC revenue target: 45% of total group revenue (from 28% in FY2025)

================================================================================
PILLAR 2: SSN PLATFORM - SAAS MODEL
================================================================================

Vision: Transform Secret Storage Network (SSN) from internal tool to
revenue-generating SaaS offering for mid-market and enterprise clients.

FY2026:
  - Q1: SSN Phase 2 GA (API, multi-vault, audit logging)
  - Q2: Pilot with 3 existing clients; gather feedback
  - Q3: SaaS pricing model design; self-service provisioning beta
  - Q4: Commercial launch; target 5 paying SaaS clients

FY2027:
  - White-label capability for financial institutions
  - Tiered pricing: Starter (NZD 2K/mo), Professional (NZD 8K/mo),
    Enterprise (custom)
  - Target: 15 SaaS clients; NZD 420K ARR from platform

FY2028:
  - Platform revenue 15% of total group revenue
  - API marketplace for third-party integrations (legal, M&A, property)
  - SOC 2 and ISO 27001 certification for platform as standalone product

================================================================================
PILLAR 3: VAULT NETWORK EXPANSION
================================================================================

Vision: Operate a distributed vault network across APAC to serve local
data residency requirements and reduce single-point-of-failure risk.

Singapore Vault (FY2026):
  - Location: Secure facility, Changi area
  - Capacity: 200 escrow slots; dual-key custody
  - Investment: NZD 185K fit-out; operational Q2 2026
  - Rationale: MAS preference for Singapore-domiciled assets; client demand

Sydney Vault (FY2027):
  - Location: TBD (Sydney CBD or North Sydney)
  - Capacity: 150 escrow slots
  - Investment: NZD 220K (est.)
  - Rationale: Australian enterprise clients; data sovereignty requirements

Wellington & Auckland (Existing):
  - Wellington: Expansion approved (Board Nov 2025); +40% capacity by Q3 2026
  - Auckland: DR and overflow; maintain current footprint

================================================================================
PILLAR 4: REGULATORY READINESS
================================================================================

Vision: Proactively meet and exceed regulatory requirements across all
jurisdictions; position for potential licensing or accreditation advantages.

FY2026:
  - Singapore: MAS registration; PDPA compliance; local legal retainer
  - NZ: FMA engagement; Privacy Act 2020 alignment
  - Australia: AUSTRAC AML/CFT if Australian entity established

FY2027:
  - SOC 2 Type II (annual); ISO 27001 recertification
  - Singapore: MAS ongoing compliance; consider MAS Digital Asset licence
    if crypto/blockchain escrow pursued

FY2028:
  - APAC regulatory map: Consolidated view of requirements across NZ, AU, SG
  - Evaluate industry certifications (e.g., PCI DSS if payment data escrow)

================================================================================
PILLAR 5: TECHNOLOGY MODERNISATION
================================================================================

Vision: Modernise infrastructure and security posture to support scale,
compliance, and platform growth.

FY2026:
  - SSN Phase 2 & 3; zero-trust network architecture
  - SIEM enhancement; 24-month log retention
  - Endpoint detection (EDR) rollout to all offices

FY2027:
  - Cloud-native vault metadata (encrypted); physical vault for keys only
  - DevSecOps pipeline for SSN; automated security scanning
  - Business continuity: Multi-region DR; tested RTO < 4 hours

FY2028:
  - Evaluate HSM upgrade; quantum-resistant crypto roadmap
  - AI/ML for anomaly detection (vault access, release patterns)
