1. DEFINITIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1.1 "Escrow Materials" means source code, object code, documentation, build
    scripts, and related intellectual property deposited by the Depositor for
    safekeeping under this Agreement.

1.2 "Beneficiary" means the party or parties designated in Schedule A who shall
    receive the Escrow Materials upon occurrence of a Release Condition.

1.3 "Release Condition" means any event specified in Clause 5.1 that triggers
    TSG's obligation to release Escrow Materials to the Beneficiary.

1.4 "Vault" means TSG's secure digital custody infrastructure located in
    Wellington, New Zealand, operated under dual-control procedures.

1.5 "Verification Event" means a scheduled or ad-hoc verification that Escrow
    Materials are complete, buildable, and match the Depositor's specifications.


2. APPOINTMENT AND ACCEPTANCE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2.1 Meridian Financial Group Ltd ("Depositor") appoints The Secrets Group (TSG)
    as escrow agent for the Escrow Materials described in Schedule B.

2.2 TSG accepts such appointment subject to the terms herein. TSG warrants that
    it holds ISO 27001 and SOC 2 Type II certifications and maintains
    appropriate insurance as specified in Clause 9.


3. DEPOSITOR OBLIGATIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3.1 The Depositor shall deposit initial Escrow Materials within 30 days of
    execution and provide quarterly updates thereafter.

3.2 All deposits must be accompanied by a Deposit Certificate signed by an
    authorised representative. Deposits are accepted by Henry Ngata, Chief
    Custodian Officer, or his designated delegate.

3.3 The Depositor warrants that it has full right to deposit the Escrow
    Materials and that no third-party rights are infringed.


4. TSG OBLIGATIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4.1 TSG shall store Escrow Materials in the Vault under dual-control access
    procedures as defined in TSG's Vault Access Policy v3.2.

4.2 TSG shall maintain environmental controls, encryption at rest (AES-256),
    and access logging. All access events are retained for 7 years.

4.3 TSG shall conduct annual Verification Events unless otherwise agreed.
    Additional verifications may be requested at the Depositor's cost.


5. RELEASE CONDITIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
5.1 Escrow Materials shall be released to the Beneficiary upon occurrence of
    any of the following, evidenced by written notice or court order:

    (a) Insolvency, receivership, or liquidation of the Depositor;
    (b) Material breach of the underlying software licence by the Depositor
        that remains unremedied for 90 days;
    (c) Cessation of business by the Depositor for a continuous period of
        180 days;
    (d) Mutual written instruction from Depositor and Beneficiary;
    (e) Court order or arbitral award directing release.

5.2 TSG shall not release Escrow Materials until it has received satisfactory
    evidence of the Release Condition and, where applicable, confirmation from
    the Depositor or its legal representative. TSG may interplead where
    conflicting claims exist.


6. FEES AND PAYMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
6.1 Annual escrow fee: NZD 18,500 (exclusive of GST), payable in advance on
    each anniversary. Includes one Verification Event per year.

6.2 Deposit fee: NZD 450 per deposit (beyond quarterly updates). Verification
    Event (additional): NZD 2,200.

6.3 Fees are subject to 3% annual increase. Payment terms: 30 days from
    invoice. Late payment may result in suspension of services.


7. LIABILITY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
7.1 TSG's aggregate liability under this Agreement is limited to the lesser of:
    (a) NZD 500,000; or (b) 12 months of fees paid in the preceding year.

7.2 TSG excludes liability for: indirect, consequential, or punitive damages;
    loss of profits; business interruption; or claims arising from the nature,
    quality, or completeness of the Escrow Materials themselves.

7.3 The liability cap does not apply to: fraud, wilful misconduct, or death or
    personal injury caused by negligence.


8. GOVERNING LAW AND DISPUTE RESOLUTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
8.1 This Agreement is governed by the laws of New Zealand.

8.2 Any dispute shall first be referred to mediation. If unresolved within 60
    days, either party may refer the matter to arbitration under the
    Arbitration Act 1996 (NZ), seated in Wellington.

8.3 The courts of New Zealand shall have non-exclusive jurisdiction.


9. TERM AND TERMINATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
9.1 Initial term: 3 years from Effective Date. Auto-renewal for successive
    12-month periods unless 90 days' written notice is given.

9.2 Upon termination, TSG shall return or destroy Escrow Materials per the
    Depositor's written instruction. If no instruction within 90 days, TSG may
    destroy after 12 months from termination.
