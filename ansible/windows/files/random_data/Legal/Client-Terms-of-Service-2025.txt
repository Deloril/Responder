3. DATA RETENTION
================================================================================

3.1 Active Escrow: Retained for the duration of the escrow agreement plus 7
    years following termination or release, unless shorter retention is
    contractually agreed.

3.2 Custodial Assets: Retained per client instructions; minimum 7 years for
    regulated industries (financial services, healthcare).

3.3 Audit Logs: Access logs, release authorisations, and security events
    retained for 10 years for compliance and dispute resolution.

================================================================================
4. DESTRUCTION PROCEDURES
================================================================================

4.1 Upon expiry of retention period or upon client written instruction, TSG
    shall destroy escrowed materials using NZS 5835:2015 compliant methods:
    - Digital media: Cryptographic erasure (NIST SP 800-88) or physical
      destruction (degaussing, shredding)
    - Physical media: Cross-cut shredding or certified destruction facility
    - Certificate of destruction provided within 14 days

4.2 Emergency destruction may be performed upon court order, regulatory
    directive, or mutual agreement. Documentation retained per Section 3.3.
