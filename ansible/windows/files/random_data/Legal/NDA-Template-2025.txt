NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between The Secrets
Group (TSG) and the undersigned Recipient (employee, contractor, or consultant)
in connection with the Recipient's engagement with TSG.


1. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
"Confidential Information" includes, but is not limited to:

(a) All contents of TSG vaults, including escrow materials, source code,
    cryptographic keys, and client-deposited assets;

(b) Client data, client identities, contract terms, and business arrangements;

(c) SSN Platform data, architecture, API specifications, and operational
    procedures;

(d) Internal policies, procedures, audit reports, and compliance documentation;

(e) Financial information, pricing, and strategic plans;

(f) Any information marked "Confidential" or which a reasonable person would
    understand to be confidential given its nature and context.


2. OBLIGATIONS OF RECIPIENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2.1 The Recipient shall hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence
    and shall not disclose, reproduce, or use such information except as
    necessary to perform duties for TSG.

2.2 The Recipient shall use at least the same degree of care to protect
    Confidential Information as the Recipient uses for their own confidential
    information of like kind, but in no event less than reasonable care.

2.3 Access to vault contents requires explicit authorisation from the Chief
    Custodian Officer (Henry Ngata) or designated delegate. Unauthorised
    access or attempted access is a material breach.


3. EXCLUSIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Confidential Information does not include information that:

(a) Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Recipient;
(b) Was rightfully in the Recipient's possession prior to disclosure;
(c) Is independently developed without use of Confidential Information;
(d) Is rightfully received from a third party without restriction.


4. RETURN AND DESTRUCTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Upon termination of engagement or upon TSG's request, the Recipient shall
promptly return or destroy all Confidential Information and certify in writing
that such action has been completed. No copies may be retained.


5. PENALTIES FOR BREACH
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
5.1 Breach of this Agreement may result in:

    (a) Immediate termination of employment or engagement;
    (b) Disciplinary action up to and including dismissal;
    (c) Civil liability for damages, including consequential losses;
    (d) Injunctive relief to prevent further disclosure;
    (e) Reporting to relevant authorities where the breach involves criminal
        conduct (e.g., theft of trade secrets, unauthorised computer access).

5.2 The Recipient acknowledges that breach may cause irreparable harm and that
    TSG shall be entitled to seek equitable relief without proof of actual
    damages.

5.3 Breach involving vault contents or client data may trigger notification
    obligations under the Privacy Act 2020 (NZ) and contractual obligations to
    affected clients.


6. TERM
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This Agreement remains in effect for the duration of the Recipient's engagement
and for a period of five (5) years following termination. Obligations regarding
trade secrets survive indefinitely until such information ceases to be a trade
secret.


7. GOVERNING LAW
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This Agreement is governed by the laws of New Zealand. Any disputes shall be
subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of New Zealand.


8. ACKNOWLEDGMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
By signing below, the Recipient acknowledges that they have read, understood,
and agree to be bound by this Agreement. The Recipient further acknowledges
receipt of TSG's Vault Access Policy, Code of Conduct, and relevant compliance
policies.
