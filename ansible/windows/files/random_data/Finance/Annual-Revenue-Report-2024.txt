EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
-----------------
The Secrets Group achieved total revenue of NZD 4,847,320 in FY2024, representing
a 23.4% year-over-year increase from FY2023 (NZD 3,927,180). Growth was driven
by SSN platform adoption, expansion of vault custodianship in Australia, and
strong performance in our core escrow services.


REVENUE BY STREAM - FY2024
================================================================================

1. ESCROW SERVICES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Description: Transaction-based fees for digital asset escrow, release-on-
             condition services, and M&A escrow arrangements.

FY2024 Revenue:     NZD 2,184,290
FY2023 Revenue:     NZD 1,892,450
YoY Growth:         +15.4%
% of Total:         45.1%

Key drivers: 47 new escrow arrangements closed; average deal size increased
12% following enterprise sales focus (Oliver Anderson, Ben). Renewal rate
among existing clients: 94%.


2. VAULT CUSTODIANSHIP
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Description: Recurring fees for secure storage of digital credentials, keys,
             legal documents, and high-value digital assets.

FY2024 Revenue:     NZD 1,456,820
FY2023 Revenue:     NZD 1,098,600
YoY Growth:         +32.6%
% of Total:         30.1%

Key drivers: 23 new vault clients; expansion of enterprise tier (Michael Latu
account management). Strong uptake from legal and financial services sectors.
Average contract value: NZD 58,200/year.


3. SSN PLATFORM LICENSING
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Description: Software-as-a-Service licensing for Secure Secret Network
             platform - API access, integration fees, per-seat licensing.

FY2024 Revenue:     NZD 847,150
FY2023 Revenue:     NZD 612,400
YoY Growth:         +38.4%
% of Total:         17.5%

Key drivers: Platform launched Q2 2023; 34 production deployments by FYE.
Asia-Pacific Trust Holdings, Meridian Financial Group among top licensees.
Hemi Tama leading platform sales.


4. CONSULTING & PROFESSIONAL SERVICES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Description: Implementation consulting, integration support, custody
             architecture design, compliance advisory.

FY2024 Revenue:     NZD 359,060
FY2023 Revenue:     NZD 323,730
YoY Growth:         +10.9%
% of Total:         7.4%

Key drivers: SSN implementation projects; pre-vault readiness assessments.
Leilani Kumar leading APAC consulting delivery.
