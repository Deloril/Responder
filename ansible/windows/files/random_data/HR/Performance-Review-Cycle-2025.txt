1. PURPOSE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This memo outlines the 2025 annual performance review cycle for The Secrets
Group. Performance reviews align individual contributions with TSG's strategic
goals, support professional development, and inform remuneration decisions.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. TIMELINE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Phase 1 - Self-Assessment:           2 Nov - 15 Nov 2025
Phase 2 - Manager Review:            16 Nov - 6 Dec 2025
Phase 3 - Calibration (Heads):       9 Dec - 13 Dec 2025
Phase 4 - 1:1 Meetings:              16 Dec - 20 Dec 2025
Phase 5 - Documentation Finalised:   6 Jan - 10 Jan 2026
Phase 6 - Remuneration Adjustments:  Effective 1 Feb 2026

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. RATING SCALE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1 - Exceptional:    Far exceeds expectations. Role model. <10% of staff.
2 - Exceeds:       Consistently exceeds expectations. 15-20% of staff.
3 - Meets:         Meets all expectations. Majority of staff.
4 - Partially:     Meets some expectations; improvement needed.
5 - Does Not Meet: Significant improvement required. PIP may apply.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
5. KPI CATEGORIES (Relevant to Escrow/Custodianship)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

VAULT OPERATIONS:
  - Custody accuracy (zero discrepancies)
  - Audit compliance (100% procedural adherence)
  - Response time to authorised requests
  - Incident reports (target: zero)
  - Dual-control protocol adherence

IT & INFRASTRUCTURE:
  - System availability (99.95% SLA)
  - Security patch compliance
  - Incident response time
  - Project delivery (SSN platform, etc.)

LEGAL & COMPLIANCE:
  - Regulatory filing deadlines met
  - Audit findings (internal/external)
  - Policy update cycle
  - Client contract review turnaround

SALES & CLIENT RELATIONS:
  - Client retention rate
  - NPS (Net Promoter Score)
  - Revenue per client
  - Escrow transaction volume

FINANCE:
  - Reporting accuracy and timeliness
  - Budget variance
  - Audit readiness
  - Cash flow forecasting accuracy

HR:
  - Onboarding completion rate
  - Training compliance
  - Turnover rate
  - Employee engagement score

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
6. DOCUMENTATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Self-assessment: Complete in Workday (or HR form if system unavailable)
- Manager assessment: Same platform
- Goals for 2026: Agreed in 1:1, documented
- Development plan: At least one development objective per staff member
- Sign-off: Employee and manager both acknowledge

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
7. CONFIDENTIALITY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Performance reviews are confidential. Ratings and feedback are shared only
with the reviewer, reviewee, and HR. Aggregated data (without names) may be
used for calibration and remuneration planning.
