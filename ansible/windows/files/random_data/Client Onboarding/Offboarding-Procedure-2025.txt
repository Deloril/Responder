================================================================================
THE SECRETS GROUP (TSG)
CLIENT OFFBOARDING PROCEDURE
================================================================================

Effective Date: 1 January 2025
Version: 2025.1
Owner: Client Services & Compliance, Wellington HQ

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. SCOPE AND APPLICABILITY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This procedure applies to all client account closures at The Secrets Group 
(TSG), including voluntary termination, contract non-renewal, and regulatory 
mandated closure. All offboarding activities must be completed within 90 days 
of notice receipt unless exceptional circumstances apply.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. NOTICE PERIOD
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

2.1 Standard Notice: 60 calendar days written notice required for voluntary 
    termination. Notice must be received in writing (signed letter or 
    authorised email from designated signatory).

2.2 Shortened Notice: May be negotiated for regulatory or force majeure 
    circumstances. Requires Director approval.

2.3 Notice Acknowledgment: Client Services must acknowledge receipt within 
    2 business days and assign offboarding coordinator.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. MATERIAL RETURN AND DESTRUCTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

3.1 Inventory: Within 5 business days of notice, generate complete vault 
    inventory for client verification. Client has 10 business days to 
    confirm or dispute.

3.2 Return Options:
    a) Physical return of media (encrypted drives) - courier arranged, 
       chain of custody documentation required
    b) Secure digital transfer to designated recipient system
    c) Certified destruction with certificate of destruction

3.3 Destruction: If client elects destruction, materials are degaussed and 
    physically destroyed per NZS 5838. Certificate issued within 5 business 
    days of completion.

3.4 Disputed Materials: Any materials subject to release conditions or 
    disputes must be segregated. Escrow agreement terms govern resolution.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. ACCOUNT CLOSURE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

4.1 Portal Access: Revoked within 24 hours of final material disposition 
    confirmation.

4.2 Vault Access: Physical access credentials invalidated upon notice 
    acknowledgment. No new deposits accepted after notice date.

4.3 API/Integration: Third-party integrations disabled within 5 business 
    days of notice.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
5. FINAL BILLING
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

5.1 Prorated Charges: Final invoice covers services through last day of 
    notice period. No refunds for pre-paid periods.

5.2 Outstanding Balances: Must be settled before material release or 
    destruction. Payment terms: 14 days from invoice date.

5.3 Disputed Invoices: Escrow materials held until billing dispute resolved. 
    Standard dispute resolution process applies.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
6. DATA DELETION CERTIFICATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

6.1 Retention Exceptions: The following may be retained for regulatory 
    compliance (minimum 7 years per AML/CFT Act 2009):
    - KYC/AML documentation
    - Transaction records
    - Correspondence relating to escrow arrangements
    - Audit trail of material handling

6.2 Deletion Scope: All client-specific operational data, portal credentials, 
    and non-mandatory records deleted within 30 days of account closure.

6.3 Certification: Client receives Data Deletion Certificate within 45 days 
    of closure, specifying retained records and retention period.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
7. REGULATORY REPORTING REQUIREMENTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

7.1 AML/CFT: Closure reported to Compliance within 5 business days. 
    Suspicious matter reporting obligations remain.

7.2 Privacy: Data processing cessation documented. Privacy Act 2020 
    requirements observed for NZ clients.

7.3 Cross-Border: For clients in Australia, Singapore, or other jurisdictions, 
    applicable regulatory notification requirements completed.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
8. OFFBOARDING CHECKLIST
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[ ] Notice received and acknowledged
[ ] Offboarding coordinator assigned
[ ] Vault inventory generated and confirmed
[ ] Material disposition method agreed (return/destruction)
[ ] Materials transferred or destroyed
[ ] Final invoice issued and paid
[ ] Portal and API access revoked
[ ] Data deletion completed (except retained records)
[ ] Data Deletion Certificate issued
[ ] Regulatory notifications completed
[ ] Client file archived
